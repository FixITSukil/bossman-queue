# Bossman Gentleman's Club Barbershop — Brand Guidelines

## Logo
- Two versions provided: square (social/avatar) and portrait (poster/print).
- The stag's head with antlers is the primary mark. The serif "BOSSMAN" wordmark sits above, "GENTLEMAN'S CLUB BARBERSHOP" + "ESTD 2024" below.
- Always keep the cream border frame around the mark when used as a standalone badge.
- Place the logo files in the repo root as:
  - `logo.png` — square version (used in the app headers)
  - `logo-portrait.png` — portrait version (optional, for print)

## Colour Palette
| Name          | Hex       | Use                                    |
|---------------|-----------|----------------------------------------|
| Forest (page) | `#0a1f14` | Page background                        |
| Pine (logo)   | `#0e2818` | Logo backdrop / deep panels            |

## Typography
- **Wordmark / headings:** Playfair Display (serif) — elegant, high-contrast. Used uppercase with wide letter-spacing for the brand name.
- **UI / body:** Jost (geometric sans) — clean, modern, pairs the spaced-caps look of "GENTLEMAN'S CLUB".
- Brand name is always UPPERCASE with generous letter-spacing (0.15–0.25em).

## Voice
Premium but approachable. Confident, masculine, classic gentleman's club feel — not flashy.

## Application
- Dark forest-green backgrounds throughout.
- Cards are a slightly lighter moss green with subtle gold/cream hairline borders.
- Gold is the call-to-action colour (buttons, "Call Next", position badges).
- Avoid pure white — use cream. Avoid bright shadows — use depth via darker greens.
